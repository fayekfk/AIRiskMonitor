## üé¨ Recording Tips for Each Shot

### Camera Movements
- **Static:** No movement, stable shot
- **Zoom In:** Slow zoom (2-3 seconds)
- **Zoom Out:** Slow zoom out (2-3 seconds)
- **Pan:** Horizontal movement (left-to-right or right-to-left)
- **Scroll:** Vertical page scroll

### Cursor Actions
- **Hover:** Move cursor over element, pause 1 second
- **Click:** Click and hold for 0.5 seconds
- **Highlight:** Circle cursor around important elements
- **Type:** Type at normal speed (not too fast)

### Transitions
- **Cut:** Instant transition (most common)
- **Fade:** 0.5-1 second fade (between major sections)
- **Dissolve:** Smooth blend (for comparisons)
