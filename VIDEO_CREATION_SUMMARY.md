## ğŸ“± Platform-Specific Tips

### YouTube:
- **Length:** 5-7 minutes (full demo)
- **Format:** Landscape (16:9)
- **Thumbnail:** High contrast with text
- **Description:** Use full template with timestamps
- **Tags:** 10-15 relevant tags

### LinkedIn:
- **Length:** 3-5 minutes (shorter version)
- **Format:** Square (1:1) or landscape
- **Captions:** Always include (most watch muted)
- **Post:** Write engaging text with hashtags
- **Engagement:** Ask questions, encourage comments

### Twitter/X:
- **Length:** 2-3 minutes max
- **Format:** Landscape or square
- **Thread:** Break into multiple tweets
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 max
- **Engagement:** Ask for RTs and feedback

### Instagram/TikTok:
- **Length:** 60-90 seconds
- **Format:** Vertical (9:16)
- **Captions:** Essential (sound often off)
- **Hooks:** First 3 seconds are critical
- **Hashtags:** 10-30 for Instagram
