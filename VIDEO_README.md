## üìä Video Specifications

### Recommended Settings:
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 FPS
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Audio:** 48kHz, AAC
- **Duration:** 5-7 minutes (full) or 2-3 minutes (quick)

### Platform-Specific:
- **YouTube:** 16:9 landscape, up to 10 minutes
- **LinkedIn:** 1:1 square or 16:9, 3-5 minutes
- **Twitter:** 16:9 or 1:1, under 2:20
- **Instagram:** 9:16 vertical, 60-90 seconds

## üÜò Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Audio quality is poor
- **Solution:** Use external microphone or record in quiet room

**Problem:** Video file is too large
- **Solution:** Export at lower bitrate or use H.264 codec

**Problem:** Screen looks blurry
- **Solution:** Record at 1920x1080, export at same resolution

**Problem:** Too nervous while recording
- **Solution:** Record in sections, edit together later

**Problem:** Features not working during demo
- **Solution:** Test everything before recording, have backup plan
